## Company SDKs and docs
- [NVIDIA Isaac ROS](https://developer.nvidia.com/isaac/ros) - GPU-accelerated ROS 2 perception for robots.
- [NVIDIA Jetson modules](https://developer.nvidia.com/embedded/jetson-modules) - Orin Nano Super through AGX Thor.
- [Hailo AI Software Suite](https://hailo.ai/products/hailo-software/hailo-ai-software-suite) - HailoRT, TAPPAS, Dataflow Compiler, Model Zoo.
- [Axelera Voyager SDK](https://github.com/axelera-ai-hub/voyager-sdk) - YAML pipelines on Apache TVM and GStreamer.
- [SiMa.ai Palette](https://github.com/sima-ai) - Software for the MLSoC and Modalix.
- [Qualcomm AI Hub](https://aihub.qualcomm.com) - 150+ pre-optimized models for Snapdragon.
- [Intel OpenVINO](https://docs.openvino.ai) - Inference across Intel CPU, NPU, and GPU.
- [MemryX SDK](https://github.com/memryx) - Open-source runtime and simulator for MX3.
- [Ambarella developer site](https://www.ambarella.com) - CVflow and the Cooper platform.

## Robot learning and VLA
- [LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot) - Hugging Face's open robot-learning library (ACT, Diffusion Policy, pi0, SmolVLA, GR00T N1.5).
- [OpenVLA](https://github.com/openvla/openvla) - Open 7B vision-language-action model.
- [Open X-Embodiment](https://robotics-transformer-x.github.io) - The cross-robot dataset behind modern VLAs.
- [VLA and Policies for Robots](https://learnopencv.com/vision-language-action-models-lerobot-policy) - LearnOpenCV conceptual primer on VLA plus diffusion policies.

## World models and simulation
- [NVIDIA Cosmos](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/ai/cosmos) - World foundation models for Physical AI (Predict, Transfer, Reason).
- [NVIDIA Cosmos on GitHub](https://github.com/nvidia-cosmos) - Open model weights and tooling.

## Papers
- [Cosmos World Foundation Model Platform (arXiv 2501.03575)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.03575) - The WFM platform paper.
- [OpenVLA (arXiv 2406.09246)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.09246) - Open VLA outperforming larger closed models.
- [Cosmos-Predict 2.5 (arXiv 2511.00062)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.00062) - Unified flow-based world generation.

## Benchmarks and analysis
- [CNX Software](https://www.cnx-software.com) - Independent hands-on reviews of edge AI hardware.
- [Phoronix](https://www.phoronix.com) - Independent Linux and accelerator benchmarking.
