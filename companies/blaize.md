## History
Founded around 2010 by former Intel engineers, Blaize **went public via a SPAC (BurTech) on Jan 14, 2025 (NASDAQ: BZAI)** at a ~$1.2B valuation — the first AI-chip startup to do so. Shares fell sharply afterward (from ~$17.50 to ~$7.55 within a week), and the company is now in a public-market scale-up phase.

## Architecture
The **Graph Streaming Processor** executes a computation graph natively, streaming data between nodes with fine-grained programmability — positioned between fixed-function NPUs and general GPUs. >10 TOPS/W on the El Cano part.
